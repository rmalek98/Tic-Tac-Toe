Game Instructions

Start by entering your name, room ID, and choosing your sign (X or O). You'll receive a link to share with your opponent, who can join the game by entering the same room ID and their name.

The winner of each round gets to restart the game. If it's a tie, the last winner can restart. After 10 rounds, players will be asked if they want to continue.
